# Product Guidelines - Muse

## Brand Messaging and Tone
- **Creative and Inspiring:** Focused on the possibilities for content creators and the "magic" of AI voice generation.

## Visual Identity and Aesthetic
- **Modern and Polished:** Utilizing Material 3 principles with subtle animations, clean typography, and a "premium" feel.

## Interaction Design Principles
- **Immediate Feedback:** Providing visual or haptic cues instantly when a user starts a conversion or adjusts a setting.
- **Contextual Guidance:** Using tooltips or subtle hints to guide new users through the voice generation process.
- **Fluid Transitions:** Ensuring the transition between text input, generation, and playback feels seamless and connected.
